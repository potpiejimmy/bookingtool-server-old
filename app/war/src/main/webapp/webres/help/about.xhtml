About

The BCON Budget Tool is a powerful budget and resource tracking web application designed to simplify the following tasks:
  • Recording personal time and effort spent on specific tasks
  • Managing the “booking templates” used for time recordings so that all bookings are compliant with SAP CATS used by Wincor corporate accounting
  • Associating all bookings to freely defined budgets (task effort estimations)
  • Keeping track of budget utilization and remaining amounts for all defined budgets
  • Exporting personal bookings into SAP CATS
  • Planning ahead on a per-month basis, creating fiscal year forecasts
  • Managing resources (beta)

The BCON Budget Tool allows you to:
  • Quickly enter your time spent on a specific task using an auto-complete input mask
  • Manage all your SAP-style booking entries on a per-day basis
  • Export your booking sheets into Excel, copy-and-paste into CATS
  • View the available budgets and their utilization
  • View per-budget forecasts based on comparison of budget depletion rate and real work progress
  • Edit your personal resource plan (beta)

As a project or team manager:
  • Manage your projects and associate projects with people and a domain
  • Manage project budgets (effort estimations) in a hierarchical tree structure
  • View all budget utilizations, budget depletion rates and their associated bookings
  • Manage all booking templates used for time recording and connect them to budgets
  • Administrate budget controls, budget plans and fiscal forecasts
  • Export resource plans for your team members (beta)

As a “system administrator”
  • Manage system users and associated roles
  • Manage system domains and associate domains with users and projects
  • Administrate projects and associated project managers
  • Define resource teams and associated team managers (beta)
